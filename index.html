About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! I'm Alex! On this page we will be displaying some samples of artwork I have done in 2022 to display the progress made. Art for me is less of a passion and more of a lifestyle. A byproduct of my existence; I create many things daily while sitting around on the train or in class. So many of this work goes unnoticed and undocumented but today I hope to show you a bit of the work I did last year! I'm excited for you to explore and let me know what you think!

Inspiration can strike anywhere! I hope it's struck you while you enjoyed some of these images now, if you wish to have artwork commissioned or contact me, please find me at alexandujar274@gmail.com. I'll email you back at a reasonable time and hopefully make a lasting impression
